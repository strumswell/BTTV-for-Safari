You can turn on BTTV for Safari’s extension in Safari Extensions preferences. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to extensions settings in Safari
  2. Enable BTTV for Safari by ticking the checkmark
  3. In Safari settings, click on Websites
  4. Scroll down to BTTV for Twitch and check allow for Twitch
